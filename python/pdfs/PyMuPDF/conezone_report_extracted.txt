State of California Department of Transportation __________________________________________________________ Date: Friday, October 26, 2018 District: 05 Santa Barbara, San Luis Obispo, Monterey, San Benito and Santa Cruz Counties Contact: Theresa Wright, (TAMC) Phone: (831) 775-0903 FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE T T TA A AM M MC C C C C CO O ON N NE E E Z Z ZO O ON N NE E E R R RE E EP P PO O OR R RT T T ROAD AND LANE CLOSURES SCHEDULED FOR OCT. 28 NOV. 3 MONTEREY COUNTY Here are the major scheduled road and lane closures for Monterey County from Sunday, October 28 through Saturday, November 3 newest information is in red. Please keep in mind that construction work is weather-dependent. Highway 1 Big Sur South Coast: October 29 November 2 Daytime paving along Highway 1 will result in traffic control between the San Luis Obispo County Line and Lime Creek Bridge form 8 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Highway Big Sur Slide: October 28 October 30 Construction work will require both directions of Highway 1 at Slide to fully close overnight for two nights, from 10 p.m. to 6 a.m., Sunday, October 28th and Monday, October 29th. The highway will reopen at 6 a.m. on Tuesday, October 30th. Motorists are advised to plan around these two emergency overnight full closures. Highway 1 Big Sur: October 29 Daytime slide removal along Highway 1 will result in traffic control at various locations between Pfeiffer Burns State Park and Partington Ridge Road from 8 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Highway 1 Bixby Creek Bridge to Coast Road (North): October 30 October 31 Daytime bridge work on Highway 1 at Point Sur will result in traffic control from 9 a.m. to 2 p.m. Highway 1 Point Sur Lighthouse- Point Sur Naval Facility: Oct. 29 November 1 Daytime utility work on Highway 1 at Point Sur will result in traffic control from 8 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. Highway 1 Arroyo Del Mar Malpaso Creek Bridge: Oct. 31 Daytime utility work on Highway 1 at Arroyo Del Mar to Malpaso Creek Bridge will result in traffic control from 9 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. on Wednesday, October 31st.
